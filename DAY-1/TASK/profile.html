Career Achievements & Milestones

The Elite Honors

  • EGOT Track: Only a Tony Award away from achieving the EGOT (Emmy, Grammy, Oscar, Tony).
  • Academy Award Winner: First hip-hop artist to win an Oscar for Best Original Song for "Lose Yourself".
  • 15-Time Grammy Winner: Tied for the record of most Best Rap Album wins in music history.
  • Emmy Award Winner: Honored for the historic Super Bowl LVI Halftime Show performance.
  • Hall of Fame Inductee: Inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2022.

Global Sales & Charts

  • Best-Selling Rapper: Over 220 million records sold worldwide.
  • RIAA History Maker: Number-one artist for certified singles in U.S. history with more than 166 million units.
  • Six Diamond Awards: One of only a few artists with multiple Diamond-certified albums and singles.
  • Billboard Artist of the Decade: Named the Artist of the Decade for the 2000s.
  • Chart-Topping Streak: 11 consecutive studio albums debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard 200.

Digital & Cultural Impact

  • Spotify Trailblazer: First hip-hop artist to surpass 100 million followers on Spotify.
  • Streaming Giant: More than 60 billion streams across digital platforms.
  • Guinness World Record Holder: Holds 13 Guinness World Records, including "Most words in a hit single" for "Rap God".
  • Dictionary Legend: The word "Stan", inspired by his song, was officially added to the Oxford English Dictionary.
