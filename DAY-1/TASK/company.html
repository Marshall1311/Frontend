Shady Records

About Company

Shady Records is an American record label founded in 1999 by world-famous rapper Eminem (Marshall Bruce Mathers III) and his longtime manager Paul Rosenberg. The company was created after the huge success of The Slim Shady LP.

The label has signed and promoted many successful hip-hop artists and has become one of the most respected record labels in the music industry. Shady Records is known for discovering talented artists, producing high-quality music, and supporting creative freedom.
